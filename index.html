ABOUT US

Welcome to our wedding place host and event organizing business! With over 10 years of experience and successful events, we offer a wide range of services to make your event unforgettable.

Our venue, located near Gurugram city, boasts a large green area, farm house, open garden, and an extra small garden perfect for intimate gatherings.

We specialize in providing expert decoration, catering, and floral services, as well as creating themed parties that will leave your guests talking. Our experienced team of professionals will work closely with you to customize our services to meet your specific needs, ensuring that every detail is taken care of.

At our venue, we strive to make your event stress-free and enjoyable. From the initial planning stages to the execution of your event, we will be there every step of the way to ensure that everything runs smoothly. Let us help you create the event of your dreams and make memories that will last a lifetime.
